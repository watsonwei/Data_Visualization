Bar Chart of Major vs. Salary

This is a bar chart that shows the salary by different majors. You may select to see the bars in stacked or grouped format. You can also re-order the bars by clicking the buttons below.

Salary Percentile by Major

This is a parallel coordinate plot that shows the salary by different majors and catgorized by 10th, 25th, 75th and 90th percentile. You may drag along an axis to filter data. Filtered data is also shown in the table below the plot.

Map of Salary Ranks

This is an interactive map that shows the salary by states or by region. Click on a state/region will display the school's average starting salary in the bar chart below in descending order. You may mouse over any state or region on the map to view more detailed salary data. You may also mouse over the bar chart to view the salary data for each school.
